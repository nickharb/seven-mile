We are a team of two who met in grad school and connected over a mutual love of telling stories online and geeking out over the latest tech. Our work has taken us around the world and includes web development, video production and data visualization.

After stints in journalism and environmental advocacy, we've recently come back together to form Seven Mile Media — a creative digital venture.

The weekend! The time you get to do all the other things you've been dreaming up. We are currently building a web application for creative people to organize and keep track of their ideas. It was born out of frustration with the lack of tools for people creating digital design or visual projects, who often have very different workflows than traditional business-related jobs. So, we decided to build something that works for us and others like us. We are really excited to share it, so stay tuned for updates! And cheers to the weekend.
